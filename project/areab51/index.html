AreaB51

Free-for-all 3rd person shooter with labyrinths, puzzles and even more.

Project overview

In AreaB51, each player has a limited amount of life and an armor. The player is also equipped with a gun that consumes energy from his energy cell each times he shoots and regains energy over time. Life won't regenerate unless the player finds syringes or food to regain health. The armor protection decreases when the player is hit and won't come back up until he finds a new one.

This project was developed by 9 students including me during a project management course.

Technologies
Python 3.2 Tkinter
